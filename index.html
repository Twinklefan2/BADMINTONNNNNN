Badminton!

badminton (noun) - /ˈbadmɪnt(ə)n/

a game with rackets in which a shuttlecock is hit back and forth across a net.

Origin

Mid 19th century: Apparently named after Badminton House in SW England, country seat of the Duke of Beaufort

History

Badminton used to be played with the players feet and was also called Ti Jian Ji. Ti Jian Ji is a traditional national Chinese sport and is also played with a shuttle. It was brought to England in 1870s, and became popular in 1929. It has been an popular olmpic sport since 1992.
